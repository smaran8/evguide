# EV Cost & Savings Guide

## Purchase Price

EVs can range from ~$25,000 to $100,000+, but the total cost of ownership often makes them competitive with gas vehicles over time.

## Federal Tax Incentives (US)

The **Inflation Reduction Act (IRA)** provides up to **$7,500** in federal tax credits for new EVs purchased from 2023 onward.

### Eligibility Requirements (as of 2024)

- Vehicle must be assembled in North America
- MSRP limits: ≤$55,000 for sedans/hatchbacks, ≤$80,000 for SUVs/trucks/vans
- Buyer income limits: ≤$150,000 (single), ≤$300,000 (married filing jointly)
- Battery component and critical mineral sourcing requirements apply

### Used EV Credit

A **$4,000 credit** (or 30% of sale price, whichever is less) is available for used EVs:
- Vehicle must cost ≤$25,000
- Buyer income limit: ≤$75,000 (single), ≤$150,000 (joint)

> **Note**: Always verify current eligibility with a tax professional or the IRS website.

## State & Local Incentives

Many states offer additional rebates, tax credits, or reduced registration fees. Examples:
- **California**: CVRP rebate up to $7,500 for eligible buyers
- **Colorado**: State tax credit up to $5,000
- **New York**: Drive Clean Rebate up to $2,000

Check [fueleconomy.gov](https://www.fueleconomy.gov/feg/taxevb.shtml) for your state's incentives.

## Fuel Cost Savings

| Scenario | Gas Vehicle | Electric Vehicle |
|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| Cost per mile (fuel) | ~$0.12–0.18 | ~$0.03–0.05 |
| Annual fuel cost (12,000 mi) | ~$1,500–$2,000 | ~$400–$600 |
| **Annual savings** | — | **~$1,000–$1,500** |

## Maintenance Savings

EVs have significantly fewer moving parts than gas vehicles:

- **No oil changes** (~$100–$200/year savings)
- **No transmission fluid, spark plugs, or timing belt**
- **Regenerative braking** extends brake pad life
- Typical EV maintenance savings: **$600–$1,000/year** vs. gas vehicles

## Total Cost of Ownership Example

| Item | Gas Vehicle | EV |
|------|-------------|-----|
| Purchase price | $30,000 | $35,000 |

| Effective price | $30,000 | $27,500 |
| Fuel (5 years) | $8,500 | $2,500 |
| Maintenance (5 years) | $5,000 | $1,500 |
| **5-Year Total** | **$43,500** | **$31,500** |
